About Umar Solar Services

MNRE & MSEDCL authorized

Umar Solar Services is an authorized solar vendor under Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) and Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) for the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana.

With over 5+ years of hands‑on industry experience, we specialize in solar rooftop design, engineering, and installation across Maharashtra. Our focus is not just installation — we deliver technically sound, structurally durable, and policy‑compliant solar solutions that are built to perform for 25+ years.
